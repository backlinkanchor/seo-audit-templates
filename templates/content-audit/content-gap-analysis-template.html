üéØ How to Use This Content Gap Analysis Template

  1. Keyword Research: Start with comprehensive keyword research using tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner
  2. Competitor Analysis: Identify top 3-5 competitors and analyze their content strategy
  3. Gap Identification: Compare your content against competitors to find gaps
  4. Prioritization: Score opportunities based on search volume, competition, and business impact
  5. Action Planning: Create detailed action plans with timelines and resource allocation
  6. Regular Reviews: Update the analysis monthly to track progress and identify new opportunities
