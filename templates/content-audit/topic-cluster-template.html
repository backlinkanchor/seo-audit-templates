üìã How to Use This Template

Step 1: Define Your Pillar Topics

Identify 3-5 broad topics that are central to your business and have substantial search volume. These will become your pillar pages.

Step 2: Research Cluster Keywords

For each pillar topic, find 8-12 related subtopics with good search volume. These will become your cluster content pieces.

Step 3: Map Keywords Strategically

Assign primary and secondary keywords to avoid cannibalization. Use the keyword mapping tab to organize your strategy.

Step 4: Create Content Calendar

Prioritize content creation based on search volume, competition, and business value. Start with pillar pages, then create cluster content.

Step 5: Internal Linking Strategy

Link cluster content to pillar pages and relevant cluster pieces. Maintain a strong internal linking structure throughout your topic clusters.

Step 6: Monitor and Optimize

Track performance of your topic clusters and identify content gaps. Use the gap analysis to find new opportunities.
